Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between var, let, and const?
var is function-scoped and hoisted, let is block-scoped and mutable, const is block-scoped and immutable.
What is the difference between map(), forEach(), and filter()?
map() returns a new array, forEach() iterates without returning, filter() returns a filtered array.
What are arrow functions, and how are they different from regular functions?
Unlike regular functions, arrow functions don’t have their own this, arguments, or prototype.
How do JavaScript Promises work?
A JavaScript Promise represents an asynchronous operation with pending, fulfilled, or rejected states.
How do closures work in JavaScript?
A closure allows a function to access variables from its outer scope even after execution.
